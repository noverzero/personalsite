Bussian Roulette

Choose your bus...and your destiny

Ghanima

This bus is named for the twin sister of Leto II AKA the Tyrant from Frank Herbert's Dune series. Mural by Brian Scott Hampton

Nymeria

This bus is named for Aria Stark's Dire Wolf in Game of Thrones. Mural by Lindee Zimmer of the Artery

Genny

This bus is a nickname for the specific model of bus, the amTran Genesis. Fun fact: BTS has lots of Genesises, but only one Genny. Mural by Pat Miblery of So-Gnar

Ygritte

This bus is named for Jon Snow's Wilding mistress from Game of Thrones. You know, nothing, Jon Snow. Mural by Jason T. Graves of Apollo Ink.

Janet

Named for the highly classified fleet of USAF passenger aircraft which transports military personnel to and from Area 51 and the Tonopah Test Range in Nevada. Janet is an acronym for "Just Another NonExistent Terminal" Mural by Remington Robinson
